About Hotel BD

An 8-minute walk from the Bangladesh National Museum, this high-end hotel next to Ramna Park is 3 km from the unfinished Lalbagh Fort and 15 km from Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport. Polished rooms feature Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and free-standing tubs. Suites add living rooms, kitchenettes and Nespresso machines. There's also butler service. Dining options consist of a chic steakhouse, a cocktail lounge and a refined restaurant, plus a cafe and a poolside bar. Other amenities include an outdoor infinity pool, a spa and a gym, as well as meeting space. Breakfast and parking are available.
